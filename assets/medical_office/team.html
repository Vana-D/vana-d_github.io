Dr. Li Liang, MD

Dr. Li Liang, MD is an American Board of Psychiatry and Neurology Board-Certified Psychiatrist and Licensed Physician in California. After completing her psychiatric residency at Creedmoor Psychiatric Center/Columbia University Presbyterian Hospital in New York City, she moved back to San Diego and practiced at San Diego County Psychiatric Hospital and Exodus Walk-in Clinic before starting ClearInsight Psychiatry. She has been in private practice for over ten years. Dr. Liang is an active member of the American Psychiatric Association and the American Academy of Clinical Psychiatrists. Dr. Liang combines her education, work experience, and commitment to learning and self-improvement to provide the best care for her patients.

She believes that good mental health is imperative for a healthy and happy life and understands that every patient’s situation is unique. Because of this, Dr. Liang takes an individualized, comprehensive, and responsive approach throughout a patient’s course of care.

Dr. Liang received her master’s degree in Physiology and medical degree from Heilongjiang University of Chinese Medicine. Dr. Liang is bilingual and can treat patients in both English and Chinese.

In her free time, she loves hiking and her favorite hike is Mt. Woodson. A place she has always wanted to travel to is Banff National Park. Her favorite holiday is Thanksgiving.

Ripal Patel, PMHNP

Ripal Patel is a PMHNP. She attended Barnes-Jewish College in St. Louis, Missouri, for her Bachelors of Science in Nursing and her Masters of Science in Nursing. Her favorite part about being a Psychiatric Nurse Practitioner is helping patients learn about their diagnosis. She believes in collaborating with patients to develop a treatment plan that fits their diagnosis and symptoms. Her favorite part about her job is seeing her patients improve. Her medical interests include major depressive disorder, anxiety disorders, obsessive-compulsive disorders, and ADHD.

In her spare time, she loves hiking. One of her favorite hikes is the Half Dome at Yosemite. She has also recently started to rock climb outdoors. Her favorite place to rock climb is in Joshua Tree National Park. She is currently learning how to cook Indian food. Her favorite holiday is Thanksgiving.


"We want all of our patients to do well. Our patients are members of our team. We are here to work with our patients to create a treatment plan that is customized to their needs."
- Ripal Patel, PMHNP
