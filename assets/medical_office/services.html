Services

Mental wellbeing is difficult. Let us help.

We treat the following mental illnesses:

  • Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD)
  • Alcohol and other illicit drug use disorders
  • Bipolar disorder
  • Dementia and other cognitive disorders
  • Depression
  • Eating disorder
  • Generalized/social anxiety disorders
  • Sleep disorder
  • Obsessive Compulsive Disorder (OCD)
  • Panic disorder
  • Post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD)
  • Schizophrenia and other psychotic disorders
  • Women’s mental health issues

Telemedicine Service Locations

We provide psychiatric care to all areas of San Diego and beyond.

As a reminder, with telemedicine, we are able to provide outpatient psychiatric and mental healthcare to beyond the general San Diego area, including Orange County, Temecula, and Riverside. Our brick-and-mortar location is conveniently located off I-15 near Mira Mesa, La Jolla, Scripps Ranch, and Poway.
