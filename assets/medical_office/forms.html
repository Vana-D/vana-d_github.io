Intake and Telepsychiatry Forms

Thank you for choosing ClearInsight Psychiatry!

Please take a look at our Intake and Telepsychiatry Forms

ATTENTION:

Please aim to fill out and turn in your new patient forms right after you schedule your appointment.

  • We require completed new patient forms before seeing you as a new patient.
  • Due to overwhelming new patient requests, we ask that you please turn in your forms at least 1 day prior to your appointment.
  • If you fail to turn in your form on time, we will be under the impression that you do not want your appointment, and we will cancel your appointment to accommodate patients with immediate needs.

Access to Printers

If you do not have access to a printer, we recommend using Adobe Reader (Windows Operating System) or Preview (Mac Operating System) to fill and sign. Further instructions can be found here.

If you do not have a physical scanner, we recommend downloading a mobile app like Adobe Scan or Microsoft Office Lens on your smartphone to scan your printed forms.

Difficulties?

If you have technical difficulty filling out and sending these forms or would like to fill out a paper version and do not have access to a printer, please give our office a call (858) 354-1304, so we can help!

When we return to in-office appointments, we will have these forms ready for you to fill out at the office. Currently, we will be closed for in-office appointments until further notice for your safety during COVID-19 pandemic.
